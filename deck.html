Ground the room

What AI is, and is not

It is
  • Predictive. It guesses the most likely next word, based on patterns it has seen.
  • A fast drafter. Quick, tireless, confident. You decide what ships.
  • A tool that reads and writes. Newer tools also create documents, images, even spreadsheets.
It is not
  • Magic. It does not understand. It pattern-matches, and it can be confidently wrong.
  • Aware of your business unless you tell it.
  • A replacement for your judgment.

Lead with the human

Safety and privacy

01
Keep a human in the loop. AI drafts, you decide. Never let it send money, sign, hire, or post on its own.
02
Keep private things out. No card or account numbers, passwords, SSNs, or customer and employee records in public AI tools.
03
Assume free tools may learn from what you type. Turn off training in settings, or use a business plan for sensitive work.
04
AI makes things up. Verify names, numbers, dates, and anything legal, tax, or medical with a person.
05
Start inside your business, not pointed at customers. A public chatbot can be wrong in public. Inside tools are easier to check.
New scams use AI voice cloning and better phishing. Verify any unusual money or access request by phone.

How to ask

The prompt framework

PartWhat to doExample
Role and GoalTell AI who it is and what to accomplishYou are my bookkeeper. Sort my expenses for last month.
StepsBreak the task into clear numbered stepsStep 1, group by category. Step 2, total each one. Step 3, flag anything unusual.
OutputSay exactly what you want backGive me a table I can paste into a spreadsheet.
EvalAsk AI to check its own workConfirm every line is counted once and the totals add up.
